terms in assasin creed and their meaning.

  1. sparta kick: is a combact ability that allows you to kick enemies off cliffs and other elevated surfaces, when executed resultes in massive damage and more often instant death.

    2. in game sparta kick is a skill that can be unlocked and upgraded as players progress higher

  2. hull: the hull refers to the structural integrity of a ship, a sturdy hull is essential of naval exploration, combat and progress in the game.
  3. revelation ability: is a skill that allows the player character to highlight and reveal hidden objects, secrects and clues in the environment.
  4. synchronization points also known as sync point are specific locations throughout the game world that players can climb and sync with the game.

    5. by synchronising these points players gain a better understanding of the game world, it reveals maps, point of intrest and so much more, it also allows you to get a panoramic view of the surrounding area and they are typically represented by tall buildings,towers and other elevated locations.

  5. visual customization:is the ability to personalize the appearance of your character,ship, equipnment.
  6. second wind: a gameplay mechanic that allows the player to recover a portion of their health, when activated it restores a moderate amount of health giving a second chance to continue figthing or escape danger.
